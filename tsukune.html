Tsukune Recipe

Tsukune is a type of Japanese chicken meatball that is typically grilled and served with a sweet and savory sauce. To make tsukune, you will need:

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sake
  • 2 tablespoons mirin
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • Bamboo skewers, soaked in water for 30 minutes

To make the tsukune:

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the ground chicken, panko breadcrumbs, egg, soy sauce, sake, mirin, sugar, ginger, garlic, and green onion until well combined.
  2. Divide the mixture into 10-12 portions, then shape each portion into a ball.
  3. Thread the chicken meatballs onto the bamboo skewers, then grill over medium heat for 6-8 minutes, turning occasionally, until cooked through.
  4. Brush the tsukune with the sauce and continue to grill for an additional 1-2 minutes, until nicely charred.
  5. Serve hot with extra sauce on the side for dipping.

Enjoy this delicious and flavorful tsukune recipe!

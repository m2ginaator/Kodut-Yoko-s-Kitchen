About Yoko's Kitchen

Yoko's Kitchen was founded in 2008 by Yoko Nakamura, a chef with a passion for bringing the delicious and healthy flavors of traditional Japanese cuisine to the UK. Since then, Yoko's Kitchen has become a popular destination for foodies looking to learn more about Japanese cooking, whether through our classes or our catering services.

Our mission at Yoko's Kitchen is to provide high-quality, authentic Japanese food that is both delicious and healthy. We use only the freshest ingredients in our cooking, and we strive to educate our customers about the many health benefits of traditional Japanese cuisine.

Okonomiyaki Recipe

Okonomiyaki is a savory Japanese pancake made with a variety of ingredients. It's a popular street food in Japan, and can be customized with different toppings and sauces.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups finely shredded cabbage
  • 1/4 cup sliced green onions
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup chopped cooked bacon or ham
  • 1/4 cup chopped shrimp
  • 1/4 cup tenkasu (tempura scraps)
  • 1/4 cup pickled ginger, chopped
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/4 cup okonomiyaki sauce or Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 cup aonori (dried seaweed flakes)
  • 1/4 cup katsuobushi (dried bonito flakes)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, baking powder, and baking soda. Whisk in water and egg until smooth.
  2. Add cabbage, green onions, carrot, bacon/ham, shrimp, tenkasu, and ginger to the batter. Mix well.
  3. Heat a non-stick frying pan or griddle over medium heat. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan for each pancake. Cook until the bottom is golden brown, then flip and cook the other side.
  4. Drizzle mayonnaise and okonomiyaki sauce over the pancakes. Sprinkle aonori and katsuobushi on top.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!

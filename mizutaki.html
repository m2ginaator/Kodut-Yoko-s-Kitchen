Mizutaki Recipe

Mizutaki is a traditional Japanese hot pot dish, typically made with chicken and various vegetables. It's a popular dish in the winter months and is often served with a dipping sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken, sliced into bite-sized pieces
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 1/2 head napa cabbage, sliced
  • 1 bunch green onions, chopped
  • 1 package enoki mushrooms, trimmed and separated
  • 1 package shirataki noodles, rinsed and drained
  • 1 block firm tofu, cut into cubes
  • 1/2 cup ponzu sauce
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1 tablespoon grated ginger

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, bring the chicken broth to a simmer.
  2. Add the chicken and vegetables to the pot and simmer until cooked through, about 10 minutes.
  3. Combine the ponzu sauce, soy sauce, mirin, and ginger in a small bowl.
  4. Serve the Mizutaki hot pot with the dipping sauce on the side.
